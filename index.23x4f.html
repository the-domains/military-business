Homeward Bound Lucca Retires, disabled veteran dog is happy and healthy!

US Military Videos & Photos / YouTube
Lucca, an 8-year-old Belgian Malinois military working dog, plays at Camp Pendleton, Calif., July 2. Lucca deployed twice to Iraq and once to Afghanistan where she was injured by an improvised explosive device. The injury led to the amputation of her left front leg and retirement from military service.
